I'm MSE Student at University of La Frontera (UFRO). Currently I collaborate in research activities. Advised by Prof. Dr. Eduardo Diez, director of the UFRO Mechatronics Laboratory.

My research interests are Automation, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence applied to engineer. I consider myself a very curious person with the spirit to learn more about new advances in science and technology.

In mid-2018 I completed an internship in Germany at Ruhr-Universität Bochum (RUB) with Ping-DAAD program, taking courses in renewable energy.

Parallel to studies I am part of DIVCiencia, an organization of scientific communication in spanish, in which we carry out activities in La Araucanía region, such as scientific talks and coffees. I am also the creator of podcasts about science at Punto Científico.
