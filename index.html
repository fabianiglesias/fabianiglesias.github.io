Fabián Iglesias-Quilodrán

My name is Fabián Iglesias-Quilodrán, I am a student of Mechanical Engineering at the University of La Frontera in Chile.

I consider myself a person very curious to learn about new advances in science and technology. I am good at working in a team and have leadership abilities. I like to keep learning a little more every day.


My main areas of interest are:

-Automation and Robotics.

-Signal analysis.

-Artificial intelligence applied to engineer.



In mid-2018 I completed an internship in Germany at Ruhr-Universität Bochum with the Ping-DAAD scholarship, taking courses in the area of ​​renewable energy.

I am currently an assistant student in the course of Manufacturing I, taught by Professor Eduardo Diez.
